# How to Delete Your Account

We are sorry to see you go. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

> **Warning:** This action is **permanent** and cannot be undone. All your data, including history, settings, and personal information, will be permanently removed from our servers.

## Instructions

1.  **Open the App**
    Launch the Nyxa app on your device.

2.  **Go to Profile**
    Tap on the **Profile** icon located in the bottom navigation bar (or the top-right corner, depending on your layout).

3.  **Access Settings**
    Scroll down to the bottom of the Profile page or look for a **Settings** (gear icon) button.

4.  **Initiate Deletion**
    Locate the **"Delete Account"** option. It is usually found at the very bottom of the list in red text.
    * *Tap "Delete Account".*

5.  **Confirm Deletion**
    A confirmation dialog will appear asking if you are sure.
    * You may be asked to re-enter your password or type "DELETE" to confirm.
    * Tap **"Confirm"** or **"Yes, delete my account"** to proceed.

## What Happens Next?
* You will be immediately logged out.
* Your account data will be scheduled for permanent deletion.
* You will receive a confirmation email stating that your account has been removed.

## Need Help?
If you are unable to delete your account or encounter an error, please contact our support team at [support@nyxa.app](mailto:support@nyxa.app) for manual assistance.
